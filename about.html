How it started?

Nonobakes originated in 2020 as a mere online dessert shop that sells cookies, cakes, and crinkles. It was the brainchild of Jebbie Dela Luz and Joeliza Dela Luz, who are currently college students across the municipality of Cainta, Rizal.

Eventually, Bento Cakes became a trend and sparked the interest of many consumers.

In the Year 2021, Nonobakes officially opened to the delight of selling highly customized and exclusively made bento cakes open for any events.
